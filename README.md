## ShopNest-Store-Power-BI-
# Description: Shop Nest Store Power BI dashboard visualizes Nexus Goods Store data (2016–2018) to drive decisions. Includes sales trends, customer behavior, 
delivery delays, payment preferences, and review scores. Enables insights on top categories, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
- The goal is to enable data-driven decision-making by analyzing sales trends, customer behavior, operational efficiency, and product performance.
Visualizations Included
- Bar Chart: Sum of price by product category (e.g., beleza_saude, relogios_presente, esporte_lazer).
- Pie Chart: Payment method distribution (credit card, boleto, voucher, etc.).
- Line Chart: Delayed orders by product category.
- Monthly Order Chart: Comparison of delayed vs. on-time orders by month.
- Review Score Charts: Count of review scores by product ID.
- Order Volume Chart: Count of orders by product ID.

 Insights Enabled
- Identify top-performing product categories and payment preferences.
- Monitor delivery performance and pinpoint delay-prone categories.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction through review scores.
- Track monthly trends in order fulfillment and sales volume

Tools Likely Used
- Power BI for dashboard creation.
- SQL for data extraction and transformation.
- Excel for preprocessing and statistical calculations.
To build a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard that visualizes key performance metrics for the Nexus Goods Store across the years 2016, 2017, and 2018. The goal is to enable data-driven decision-making by analyzing sales trends, customer behavior, operational efficiency, and product performance.
